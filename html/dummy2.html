Program Infaq Ramadan KOPELADAR

10 March 2026 | Kota Bharu & Gua Musang

Sharing Blessings with the Community

In line with our mission to foster a culture of integrity and community support, KOPELADAR has launched its annual Infaq Ramadan program. This initiative aims to provide essential support to over 500 families across Kelantan during the holy month.

Distribution Pillars

  • Food Baskets: Distribution of rice, oil, and dry goods to asnaf families.
  • Education Support: Special vouchers for children of cooperative members.
  • Mosque Contributions: Funding for Iftar programs in rural districts.

How to Contribute

Members can donate via our digital portal or at any KOPELADAR branch.
