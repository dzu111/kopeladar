Advancing Strategic Growth for 2026

The 52nd Annual General Meeting of KOPELADAR serves as a cornerstone for our future, bringing together members and the Board of Directors to finalize the strategic roadmap for the 2026/2027 financial year.

This year's focus remains on expanding our business portfolios in property and retail, while maintaining our core values of integrity and transparency.

Key Agenda Highlights

  • Review of 2024 Financial Excellence & Asset growth.
  • Approval of new property ventures in Gua Musang.
  • Digitalization of cooperative membership services.
